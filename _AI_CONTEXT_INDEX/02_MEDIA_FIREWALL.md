### Assessment: Fitts' Claim — "Epstein Allocated Funds to Crypto/Programmable Money Projects"

#### Verdict: ⚠️ PARTIALLY SUPPORTED — Narrower Than Claimed

**What is supported by DOJ documents:**
1. Epstein invested $3M in Coinbase (2014) via USVI shell company — ✅ verified
2. Epstein invested in Blockstream indirectly via Joi Ito's fund (2014) — ✅ verified
3. Epstein donated $525K to MIT's Digital Currency Initiative, which funded Bitcoin Core developers — ✅ verified
4. Epstein hosted private meetings at his Manhattan townhouse where Bitcoin and crypto regulation were discussed with Brock Pierce and Larry Summers — ✅ verified
5. Epstein expressed interest in Bitcoin taxation and regulatory frameworks in internal emails — ✅ verified

**What is NOT supported by DOJ documents:**
1. No evidence of strategic "allocation" of funds to "programmable money" as a deliberate category or program
2. No documented transfers to CBDC development programs
3. No evidence of protocol-level influence on Bitcoin or any other cryptocurrency
4. No evidence connecting Epstein to the Satoshi Nakamoto identity (debunked)

**Assessment**: Fitts' claim that "Epstein allocated funds to crypto and programmable money projects" is **partially true** but **significantly overstated** as presented. The DOJ files document Epstein as an **early investor and networker** in the crypto space — specifically Coinbase, Blockstream, and MIT's Digital Currency Initiative — but his role was that of a **financier and social connector**, not an architect or strategic allocator of a "programmable money" agenda. Fitts' broader framing — that Epstein was building a "control grid" through crypto investments — is her **interpretive framework** layered on top of documented but more limited financial connections.

**Repository-specific observation**: Fitts' framing channels audience anger toward "crypto" and "programmable money" as abstractions while naming none of the specific intermediaries (1789 Capital, Silver Lake, MGX, PIF, Omeed Malik) documented in this repository's Media Firewall analysis. This pattern is consistent with the **controlled disclosure** assessment in `_AI_CONTEXT_INDEX/sources/2026-02-27_Carlson_Fitts_Control_Grid.md`.
