### Russia's Venue Preference

Russia initiated the push to move talks from Geneva back to Abu Dhabi, claiming that Switzerland had "lost its status of a neutral power." Russian officials argued that Geneva's proximity to EU and NATO countries undermined the impartiality of the venue. The US reportedly backed the shift to Abu Dhabi, adding legitimacy to Russia's request.

### Russian Response

Russian military bloggers described the Starlink cutoff as a communications "catastrophe." Pro-war bloggers on Telegram reported command and control collapse, forcing a return to radios and wired communications. One Russian military blogger cautioned followers to "not believe in fairy tales" about quick fixes for the communications gap.

Sources: [Moscow Times](https://www.themoscowtimes.com/2026/02/05/pro-war-bloggers-report-starlink-outage-on-ukrainian-front-line-a91875), [Kyiv Independent](https://kyivindependent.com/dont-believe-in-fairy-tales-russia-scrambles-for-front-line-signal-after-starlink-cut-off/), [RFE/RL](https://www.rferl.org/a/russia-s-starlink-setback-will-moscow-snap-back-from-a-new-battlefield-blow-in-ukraine-/33675248.html)
