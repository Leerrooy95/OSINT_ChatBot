## Event Summary

On February 28, 2026, the United States and Israel launched coordinated military strikes against Iran. The US designated its campaign "Operation Epic Fury" and Israel designated its component "Operation Lion's Roar." Strikes targeted strategic locations across Iran including Tehran, Isfahan, Qom, Karaj, and Kermanshah. Iran retaliated under "Operation Fateh Khyber," launching ballistic missiles at US military facilities hosted by Gulf Arab states.
